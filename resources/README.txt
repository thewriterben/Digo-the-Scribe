Place Digital Gold Co resource PDFs here.

Required files (place them in this directory):
  • battle_plan.pdf      — The Digital Gold Co Battle Plan
  • beyond_bitcoin.pdf   — "Beyond Bitcoin" by John Gotts

These files are intentionally excluded from version control (.gitignore)
to protect sensitive business information.  Obtain them from Operations
Manager Benjamin J. Snider and place them here before running Digo.

Additional resources (e.g. CFV-Metrics-Agent data, website links) can be
loaded at runtime via:
    digo load-resource --name "Resource Name" --path /path/to/file.pdf
